The Harmonic Series

The Harmonic series is the sequence of running totals, or partial sums of the reciprocals.  That might sound scary, but its really quite simple. A partial sum of an infinite sequence is the sum of all terms of the sequence up to a point. In the case of the reciprocals (1, 1/2, 1/3, 1/4) this would be:

1,
or  1+1/2,
or  1+1/2+1/3,
or  1+1/2+1/3+1/4,
etc.

These partial sums are themselves a sequence:

1,   1+1/2,   1+1/2+1/3,   1+1/2+1/3+1/4,... 


Which is sometimes written as:

1 + 1/2 + 1/3 + 1/4 + ...
