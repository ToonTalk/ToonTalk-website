Activity: Matching motion representations

This task involves students matching up four types of corresponding representations of lunar lander motion events: a narrative description, an up position-time graph, an up speed-time graph, and a ToonTalk box/ sensor description. The word document is available here. Simply print the document (cardboard or thick paper good), cut around the cards, shuffle and give to students. It works well with groups of 2-4 students, and can be video/ audio taped for subsequent analysis. A further extension is to ask students how they could design a similar game for another student group.

The Excel file from which the graphs were created is available here, in case you might want to change the graphs.
