Lunar Lander notebook

There are 4 things in the Lunar Lander notebook:

  1. A picture of a Lunar Lander (rocket).
  2. An acceleration behaviour, to make an object accelerate up or down at a given rate.
  3. A tool for copying the values of a sensor (e.g. Up or Up Speed) to the clipboard, so that the data can be pasted into Excel and plotted against time as a graph.
  4. A Lunar Lander with booster behaviour that applies a force to the lander when the 'Shift ' key is pressed. The amount of resulting acceleration depends on the boost force and on the mass (size) of the lander. The lander also makes a noise and displays flames when the 'Shift' key is pressed.
