    When your challenge is responded


    If the garden that someone posted is the same thing as your original one
        say something nice, upload your original, and think of a tougher challenge.

    If the responder has not uncovered your garden
        publish your garden to show that your garden is different.

     

    Publishing challenges, answers and solutions


    Go back to the Guess my Graden main page and follow the instructions below.

    To publish a challenge    : comment this page selecting the option "other", writing "Challenge by YOUR NAMES" in the form, and clicking the button GO. Then upload your Challenge Notebook   in your comment and save it.

    To respond to a challenge: locate the challenge in this page; select the option "other" below the challenge writing "Response by YOUR NAMES" in the form and clicking the button GO. Then upload your Random Garden in your comment and save it.

    To give the solution of your challenge: locate the challenge in this page; select the option "other" below the challenge writing "Solution by YOUR NAMES " in the form and clicking the button GO. Then upload your Random Garden in your comment and save it.
