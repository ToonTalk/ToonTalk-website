Move and turn

What does it do?

Contains two ways of moving left-right, and turning upon collision with other objects.
 

How do I use it?

To use the tools you need two versions of a picture, one looking to the right and the other looking to the left.
 

How does it work?

The two versions work slightly different:

  • The tiger takes steps and has two behaviours (that must be used together.)
    1. Take steps
      • You can change the size of the steps in the "how much" hole of the box.
      • How often the steps are taken can be changed in the right "when"-hole
    2. Flip picture and reverse speed when hitting something.
      • Change the pictures of the tiger in the "left" and "right" holes of the robot's box.

 

  • The killer whale moves smoothly at a constant speed, and also has two behaviours
    1. Move to side
      • To change the speed, just change the number in the "set speed" whole in the box.
    2. Flip
      • Just change the pictures in the "right" and "left" holes in the flip-robot's box.
