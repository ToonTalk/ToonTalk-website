More TT Sweeper Bot - (RandomBot 2D)

What does it do?

A vehicle that moves in a 2D area and sweeps balls away, or disintegrates them. Movements can be random or guided using arrows keys.
 

How do I use it?

Copy as many balls as you like in the white field, then press the space bar to activate the vehicle. In the random versions the vehicle will move randomly on its own, in the controlled version you will have to move it hitting the arrow keys. In the version driven by a Random Garden you drive it changing the numbers of different arrows in the Random Garden.
 

How does it work?

It executes either a random walk, or a guided walk.

What can I use it for?

Simulating random walks, build play games with random walk, or with vehicles controlled via arrow keys. In combination with the Bounce Random Garden it can be used as a random generator.
Used in the TT Sweeper Bot activity.

Simple sweeper: the vehicle moves randomly sweeping balls out of its territory.




Sweeper eater: the vehicle moves randomly and disintegrates balls when it meets them.




Controlled sweeper: the vehicle's movements are guided using the key arrows.
When it meets a ball it destroys it.




Random Garden Sweeper: the vehicle moves randomly according to the sample space defined by its random garden which can be modified in real time by the user.
