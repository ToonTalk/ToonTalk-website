Publishing challenges, answers and solutions

  • To pubilsh a challenge: add a comment to this page selecting the option "other", writing "Challenge by YOUR NAMES" in the form, and clicking the button GO. Then upload your Challenge Notebook
  • To respond to a challenge: locate the challenge in this page; select the option "other" below the challenge writing "Response by YOUR NAMES" in the form and clicking the button GO. Then upload your Random Garden in your comment and save it.
  • To give the solution of your challenge: locate the challenge in this page; select the option "other" below the challenge writing "Solution by YOUR NAMES " in the form and clicking the button GO. Then upload your Random Garden in your comment and save it.
