TT Sweeper Bot

What does it do?

The robot moves randomly according to the sample space defined in the garden. You can put balls or bombs in the robot's field and they will disappear once the robot touches them.

 

How do I use it?

Copy and drop balls in the field of the robot. Press the space bar pointing at the field to let the robot start (if it doesn't move, press start also pointing at the garden). You can change the probabilistic movements of the robot biasing its movements by changing the numbers of arrows in the garden.

 

How does it work?

At each step, it extracts randomly, a direction for the garden and makes a step in that direction.
 

What can I use it for?

Experience probabilistic driving, play conjecture and verification games concerning probability and sample spaces and controlling of random events.
