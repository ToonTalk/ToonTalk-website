ToonTalk is my attempt to make programming accessible to all

  • Computational abstractions are mapped to concrete analogs (without loss of expressivity).
  • Programming is done with concrete examples and then generalized.
  • The interface, look and feel, help system, and tutorials are based upon computer and video games.
  • Virtual communities for support, sharing, etc.
  • Underlying computation model is based upon autonomous concurrent coordinated processes.
