Basic Hints for ToonTalk Puzzles

When playing ToonTalk's puzzle game, the first thing you have to do is move the mouse to enter the rocket by walking into the door. Marty will greet you and explain the first problem. You then need to walk back through the rocket door and walk over to the door of the house. Once inside click a mouse button to sit down. Then build whatever Marty needs using a mouse button to pick up and drop things. If you mess up, just pick up the bomb and hit the space bar. If you need help, walk back to Marty. Each time you walk into the room or bump into him, you'll get some new hints from him. When you do think you have built what Marty needs, pick it up, and stand up by pressing the 'Esc' button on the keyboard. Then walk back to the rocket.

Good luck!
