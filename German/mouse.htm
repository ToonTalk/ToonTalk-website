Using your Mouse in ToonTalk

To simplify things, usually all the mouse buttons mean the same thing in ToonTalk. You can assign accelerators to the middle and right buttons. The right button can be assigned so that clicking it while holding a tool "uses" it. In other words, it acts like the "space bar". The right button can also be used to flip over text, numbers, and pictures. The middle button can be assigned to any key. The easiest way to change this is to select the option you want after clicking on "Set Options" after starting ToonTalk.

The default assignment for the middle button is '-' for turning pages backwards and cycling backwards through many remote controls. This can be overridden in your toontalk.ini file.
