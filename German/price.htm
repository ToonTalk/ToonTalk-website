We are sometimes asked why ToonTalk costs $49.95 (in the North and South America and much of Asia) when many excellent software titles for children cost about half that. If ToonTalk were simply a game, its price would be much less. If ToonTalk were simply a tutoring program that teaches computer programming concepts, its price would be much less. But ToonTalk is a game, a tutor, and a tool.  And programming tools usually cost much more than $49.95.
