Hints for Using Robots in ToonTalk Puzzles

A robot in ToonTalk can be trained by giving him a box. You'll then enter his thought bubble. Whatever you do, the robot will remember. Some robots can only remember a few steps. When a robot's memory fills up, you leave his thought bubble. You can leave whenever you want by pressing the 'Esc' button. Once a robot is trained you can give him a box. If the box matches what's in his thought bubble, then he'll repeat what he was trained to do. If the box doesn't match what he's thinking of, then Dusty will appear. You can use Dusty to remove stuff from the robot's thought bubble so he isn't so fussy. A good habit is to remove only enough to make the robot happy with what you want to give him.

Some robots have a magic wand that only they can use. You'll have to train the robot to use the wand for you.
