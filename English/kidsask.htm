"ToonTalk is a video game for making video games."

-- David Kahn, age 10.

"It's an animated world where kids can make, run, debug, and trade programs."

-- Ken Kahn, David's dad and inventor of ToonTalk

You start off flying a helicopter. After you land you can walk into houses and use animated tools and characters to add to your town or build games.

In ToonTalk, you fly your helicopter into a city you've designed.

I think you really should check out the animated tools and characters .
