Pumpy the Bike Pump can make things get bigger and smaller.

If you press the space bar while holding him he'll pump air into whatever is under the end of his hose.

If you press the button on his chest it can change from 'B' for big, to 'W' for wide, 'N' for narrow, 'L', for little, 'S' for short, 'T' for tall, and 'G' for good size. SHOW ME

For advanced users there are special keyboard commands to quickly make Pumpy make things smaller, taller, shorter, wider, and thinner.

You can call for Pumpy to jump into your hand by pressing the F3 key.
