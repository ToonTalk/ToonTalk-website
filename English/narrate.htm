Narration for ToonTalk demos

Narrations for the demos are available on the CD-ROM versions of ToonTalk. The Introductory Tour Demo with US English narration can be played by dragging this into ToonTalk. (It is 5MB so you may need to be patient.) Or you can save it in the ToonTalk\Demos\US folder and then this narrated version will be used whenever the Introductory Tour demo is run.

The other narrated demos and the British English, Swedish, and Portuguese versions are only available on CD-ROMs.
