How to Contribute ToonTalk Programs to the Web

Since product version 1.1 or Beta 18 there are easier ways of saving programs than the following.

If you want to contribute something nice you made in ToonTalk do the following. Go to page 4 of the notebook that lives in Tooly, the Toolbox. Take out that notebook and go to page 30 where you'll find a clipboard remote control. If something is already there, just use Dusty to vacuum it out. Then drop a copy of what you want to contribute on the clipboard. Bammer should come and smash it into the clipboard. Now start up your email program (you can do it by clicking here) and paste the program into the body of the message. (Most mailers have an "Edit" menu where paste is one of the options.)

A tip. If you have many things, then put them all in a blank notebook (you'll find one on page 12 of the notebook that lives in Tooly) and then drop that on the clipboard.

Thanks for your help.
