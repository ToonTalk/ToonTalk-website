What to do if Marty doesn't speak in ToonTalk

If you have installed ToonTalk from a CD-ROM or you have voices installed on your computer, then Marty should be able to speak. Here are some of the things that can go wrong:

If ToonTalk can't find the voices. Click on 'Set Options' and there should be one about speech and another listing available voices. Select the voice you want to hear. If no voices are listed then you may need to install Microsoft's SAPI 4a text-to-speech extensions.
