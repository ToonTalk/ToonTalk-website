ToonTalk 2 Fact Sheet

 

 

 

ToonTalk 2 Description

ToonTalk 2, published by Animated Programs, is the first animated computer programming tutorial for children. Designed to teach the basics of programming, including constructing, debugging and running programs, in a fun-filled animated interactive world, ToonTalk 2 gives children the knowledge and skill sets to program their own creations. In ToonTalk 2, programming is made easy for children to understand and implement. They learn while enjoying the environments of ToonTalk 2, which include puzzle games, problem solving and narrated demonstrations.

 

ToonTalk 2 Key Features

—    Teaches children computer programming in a fun and supportive environment.

—    Educational skills include puzzle solving, logic problems and critical thinking.

—    Three levels of play including narrated demonstrations.

—    Easy to use and understand.

—    Terrific price point at $24.95.

—    Children can create their own games and programs and share them with friends.

—    Internet enabled and browser integrated.

 

ToonTalk 2 Requirements and Availability

ToonTalk 2 requires: Windows 95/98/NT4/2000/ME/XP. A CD-ROM drive is required for installation. A sound card is highly recommended.

 

In-depth information and technical details can be found at www.toontalk.com/English/press.htm.

 

ToonTalk 2 is currently available for Windows at $24.95 and can be ordered online at www.toontalk.com or by calling 800-317-9830. Versions are also available in Japanese, UK English, Swedish, European Portuguese and Brazilian Portuguese.
