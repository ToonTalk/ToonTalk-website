How to Contribute Things to ToonTalk

ToonTalk would be even better if you helped. There are lots of things you can do.

Share things you've made in ToonTalk. If you make some neat little (or big) thing in ToonTalk you can get it posted on the World Wide Web for others to enjoy.

Make puzzles for others to solve. When you click on "Play Game" after starting ToonTalk you can solve lots of puzzles that come with ToonTalk. But you can make your own and share them with others.

Make your own demos. When you click on "See Demos" you see about 10 demos that comes with ToonTalk. You can make your own as well.

Share your suggestions for improving ToonTalk. You can either send suggestions to support@toontalk.com or join the ToonTalk email discussion list and discuss them there.
