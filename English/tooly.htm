Tooly the Toolbox

Tooly the Toolbox is full of neat things to play with.

Tooly follows you wherever you go. Inside of Tooly you'll find

  • numbers and text pads
  • boxes and nests
  • scales and robots
  • trucks and bombs

Also Dusty, Pumpy, the Magic Wand, and your notebook travel around inside Tooly.

Tip. If want Tooly and the things inside to be bigger or smaller, you can use Pumpy to change his size.

Tip. You can call for Tooly to run to where you are sitting by pressing F6.
