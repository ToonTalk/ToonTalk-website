Does ToonTalk Sacrifice the Power of Abstract Thinking?

ToonTalk programmers can think concretely because

  1. The construction and execution of programs are interleaved.

  2. Programs are constructed by operating upon an example and then removing details.

  3. Computational abstractions have been replaced by "concretisations".

While this is great for children, especially young children, what if anything has been lost?
