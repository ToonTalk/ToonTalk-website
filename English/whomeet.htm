Who will you meet in ToonTalk?

The very helpful Marty the Martian is the first one you'll meet. Some tools you can use are Dusty the Vacuum , Pumpy the Bike Pump , and Maggie the Magic Wand . And Tooly the Toolbox is full of neat stuff. Bammer the Mouse smashes things but is really useful. And you can train robots to do pretty much anything a computer can do. And one of the characters is you. And there's more!
