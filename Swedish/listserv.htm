The ToonTalk Email Discussion Group

Toontalk@yahoogroups.com is an email list is for discussing ToonTalk. Use it to ask questions, make suggestions, air complaints, help other users, and post ToonTalk programs. Remember that everyone on the list will see your postings so be polite and keep to the topic. Bug reports can be sent here or to support@toontalk.com.
